Get to know the little lady herself

“If graphic design was a party, Vienna Marie would be the first one on the dance floor with a plate of cake in one hand and a camera in the other.”

Known for her bubbly personality and charismatic charm, Vienna Marie is an emerging multi-disciplinary graphic designer, photographer and videographer from Sydney, Australia. Currently studying Visual Communications (Graphic Design), she has a knack for finding the most beautiful features within the most simplest of settings. Evident through her many involved projects and collaborations with such companies like Dermaesthetics Skincare, Kino Sydney and youtube personalities Zorsy and Redscope films, she specialises in both freelance and corporate visual communications. From the handling of a paintbrush, to a snap of a camera, down to a massive performance in front of hundreds, her strong creative and performing arts background has enabled herself to find joy within any artistic activity - which is something that she hopes her viewers can gain as well.
